🩺 Part 1: Current Website Diagnostic & Opportunities

Your site is built on a robust, highly scalable stack: WordPress + WooCommerce, optimized with WP Rocket and managed with Yoast SEO. This is excellent, as it allows us full control over the technical structure without requiring a costly platform migration.

However, our deep crawl has identified several critical, easily fixable search bottlenecks that are currently costing you direct revenue:

1.1 Homepage H1 Tag Correction (🔴 Critical)

  • Current State: The only H1 tag on your homepage is <h1>LAS MÁS VENDIDAS</h1> (which sits above the best-sellers product section).
  • The Search Bottleneck: Google's crawlers interpret the H1 as the primary theme of the page. Currently, Google understands your store's main topic as "best sellers" rather than your prestigious brand name and curated collection.
  • Our Fix: We will update this site-wide. Your homepage H1 will be modified to an elegant, custom-styled luxury title: <h1>Niche Perfumes — Luxury, Artistic & Exclusive Fragrance Boutique</h1>.

1.2 Empty Image Alt Texts (🔴 Critical Traffic Loss)

  • Current State: Almost all live product images on your homepage, brand grids, and category listings have empty alt tags: <img src=".../sirene-prive.webp" alt="">.
  • The Search Bottleneck: Google cannot "see" images. Without Alt texts, your stunning luxury bottles are completely invisible in Google Images searches. High-end buyers search heavily on Google Images for fragrance aesthetics, unboxing reviews, and bottle presentation.
  • Our Fix: We will implement our proprietary image naming and Alt-text mapping protocol. Every image will follow an elegant, keyword-rich luxury template: <img src="sirene-prive.webp" alt="Sirène Privée by Fragrance du Bois — Luxury Eau de Parfum 75ml">.

1.3 Viewport Image Loading & Core Web Vitals (🟡 Performance)

  • Current State: WP Rocket's lazy-loading is active, but it is currently applied too aggressively, lazy-loading the main homepage slider and the first row of products (the viewport visible "above the fold").
  • The Search Bottleneck: Delays the loading of the main visual block. This severely penalizes your Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) metric, a critical ranking factor in Google's PageSpeed algorithm.
  • Our Fix: We will add precise exclusion rules in WP Rocket for the first 4–6 visible viewport images, marking them with high-priority tags for instant rendering: <img src="hero-banner.jpg" loading="eager" fetchpriority="high" alt="Exclusive Niche Perfumes Collection">.

1.4 Structured Schema Markup (🔴 Major Disadvantage)

  • Current State: No Product Schema, Offer Schema, LocalBusiness Schema, or Breadcrumb Schema is active on nicheperfumes.net.
  • The Search Bottleneck: When users search for your products, your competitors appear in Google with price tags (€245.00), review stars, and "In Stock" labels, capturing all the clicks. Your listings appear as flat, generic text lines.
  • Our Fix: We will build and inject structured JSON-LD Schema across your entire catalog, ensuring your listings stand out as prominent, visual "Rich Snippets" on Google.

1.1 Corrección de Etiqueta H1 en la Homepage (🔴 Crítico)

  • Estado Actual: El único tag H1 en su página de inicio es <h1>LAS MÁS VENDIDAS</h1> (que se ubica sobre el carrusel de best-sellers).
  • El Cuello de Botella: Los rastreadores de Google interpretan el H1 como el tema central del sitio. Actualmente Google entiende que la temática principal de su portal es "las más vendidas", en lugar de su prestigioso nombre de marca y colección curada de perfumes.

1.2 Textos Alt de Imágenes Vacíos (🔴 Crítico - Pérdida de Tráfico)

  • Estado Actual: Prácticamente todas las imágenes de producto de la home, cuadrículas de marca y listados tienen etiquetas alt vacías: <img src=".../sirene-prive.webp" alt="">.
  • El Cuello de Botella: Google no puede "ver" imágenes. Sin textos Alt descriptivos, sus hermosas botellas de lujo son 100% invisibles en las búsquedas de Google Imágenes. Los compradores premium buscan intensamente en Google Imágenes por estética, frascos o empaques.

1.3 Carga de Imágenes sobre el Viewport y Core Web Vitals (🟡 Rendimiento)

  • Estado Actual: La función de Lazy-loading de WP Rocket está activa, pero se aplica de forma demasiado agresiva, lazy-loadeando el slider principal del banner de la home y la primera fila de productos visibles.
  • El Cuello de Botella: Esto retrasa notablemente el despliegue del bloque visual superior, penalizando severamente la métrica Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), factor de posicionamiento clave de Google.
  • Nuestra Solución: Añadiremos reglas de exclusión en WP Rocket para las primeras 4–6 imágenes de la parte superior, marcándolas con etiquetas de alta prioridad para renderizado instantáneo: <img src="hero-banner.jpg" loading="eager" fetchpriority="high" alt="Colección Exclusiva de Perfumes de Nicho">.
